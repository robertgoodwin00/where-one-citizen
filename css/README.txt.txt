Thanatophobia
by Robert Goodwin copyright 2022


A chatbot/game about helping a young woman with a mental illness.


How to play:
Just chat by typing English into the text input field.


Hints:
To complete the game you must discover 2 facts and complete 1 action. One fact concerns what Madeline is now seeing in the present. One fact concerns her past. They are related. The 1 action becomes available once you have discovered the 2 facts and is a command you give to Madeline.
