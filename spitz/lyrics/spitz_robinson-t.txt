
             
            Robinson 
            by Spitz 

Atarashii kisetsu wa
Naze ka setsunai hibi de
Kawara no michi wo jitensha de
Hashiru kimi wo oikaketa
Omoide no REKOODO to
Oogesa na EPISOODO wo
Tsukareta kata ni burasagete
Shikametsura mabushisou ni
--
For some reason, the new season 
Brings painful days
I followed you running down
The dry river bed on my bike
With the records of my memory
And exaggerated episodes
Hanging on my tired shoulders
I grimace at the brilliance


Onaji serifu onaji toki 
Omowazu kuchi ni suru you na
Arifureta kono mahou de tsukuri ageta yo
--
As if thoughtlessly saying
The same lines at the same time
With that mundane magic spell, I built it for you


Dare mo sawarenai futari dake no kuni
Kimi no te wo hanasanu you ni
Ooki na chikara de sora ni ukabetara
Ru ra ra uchuu no kaze ni noru
--
A land for just the two of us that noone can touch
So that I won't let your hand go
If I could float in the sky with a large power
Ru ra ra, I'll ride the winds of space


Katasumi ni suterarete
Kokyuu wo yamenai neko mo
Dokoka nite iru daki agete
Muriyari ni hoho yoseru yo
Itsumo no kousaten de 
Miageta marui mado wa
Usuyogoreteru 
Girigiri no mikazuki mo boku wo miteta
--
Abandoned in the corner
Even the cat who can't stop panting
Resembles you somehow, and I pick it up
And snuggle it forcibly to my cheek
At the usual crosswalk
The round window that I looked up at
Was grimy all over
And even the new moon watched me


Machibuseta yume no hotori 
Odoroita kimi no hitomi
Soshite bokura ima koko de umare kawaru yo
--
Near the edge of the dream where I lay in ambush
I saw your surprised eyes
And then we'll be reborn here


Dare mo sawarenai futari dake no kuni
Owaranai uta baramaite
Ooki na chikara de sora ni ukabetara
Ru ra ra uchuu no kaze ni noru
--
A land for just the two of us that noone can touch
Scattering unending dreams
If I could float in the sky with a large power
Ru ra ra, I'll ride the winds of space


Ooki na chikara de sora ni ukabetara
Ru ra ra uchuu no kaze ni noru
Ru ra ra uchuu no kaze ni noru
--
If I could float in the sky with a large power
Ru ra ra, I'll ride the winds of space
Ru ra ra, I'll ride the winds of space
