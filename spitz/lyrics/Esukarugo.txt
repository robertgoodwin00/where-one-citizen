Escargot


Dame da na, gomi da na
Sarigenai kotoba de tokeru kokoro
Kowamote hagarete shikata ne to naite mimi o sumasu 
kodoku na makigai no soto kara
Fuzaketa  gitaa no oto ga kikoeru yo


You’re no good, only trash
You melt my heart with such dry words.
It’s no use, I cry, I’ll strip the fear. Listen up
I can hear a toy guitar from outside a lonesome sea shell.


Yukemuri hidamari atarashii hikari ni
Sugata sarasu odayaka na samusa
Butsukeatta koma wa itsuka tomaru
Karehamai koi no ame ga furu
Yorenagara kasoku shite iku yo

Steam, Sunshine, there’s a calm cold.
My profile is bleached from the brand new light.
The crash induced coma has to end sometime.
Dry leafs fall. The rain I love falls.
I’ll race into complication.

Hanii kimi ni todokitai 
Mou sukoshi de michikara
Soreteku nanimo mayouwanai
Oikakeru zara zara no sekai e

Honey, I want to reach you.
Just a little further and I will wander off the path.
Nothing astray, I will chase this uneven world

Tsumaranai kudaranai mezameta atama ga
Ina to sakebu hajikete tobidete koko dake wa
Hapidei no dokuro tatoo kanu kogu tsukarete mo
Yamezuni ano shima ga chikazuite kuru made

No fun, it’s dull. No! Screams my head, awake.
Bursting, poping, only here is happy-day’s skull tatoo.
Though I’m tired, I won’t stop paddling till I get close to that island.

Hanii kimi o jamashitai gomen nasai
Osokare hayakare subete wakaru hazu
Shoujiki na zara zara no sekai e

Honey, I just want to complicate you.
I’m sorry, sooner or later we’re supposed to understand everyting.
We’re headed for the truly uneven world.
