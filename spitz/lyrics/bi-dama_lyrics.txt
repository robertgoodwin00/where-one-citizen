   Bi-dama
   ("Marbles") by Spitz

   words by Kusano Masamune


 
  omae no saigo wo miteyaru
  yawarakai moufu ni kurumatte
  yukkuri ukandara namida no hoshi ni natta

I'll watch your death.
Wrapped up in a soft blanket,
if you float slowly, you will be at the star of tears.

--

  douse PACHIN to hibiwarete
  minna yume no you ni kiesatte
  zutto fukai yami ga hirogatteiku nda yo

Anyhow, cracking open with a snap
everybody fades away like a dream.
A dense darkness spreads on continually.

--

  TAMASHII korogase
  CHIIPA CHIIPA CHIIPACHIIPA
  TAMASHII korogase niji ga kakaru koro ni

Roll over the spirit, chiipa chiipa chiipachiipa.
Roll over the spirit, around the time when a rainbow was visible.

--

  ore wa kurutteita no ka na
  sorairo no NAIFU wo te in motte
  makka na chi no umi wo tobikoetekita nda yo  

Was I mad I wonder?
Holding an azure knife in my hand
I flew across a sea the color of crimson.

--

  TAMASHII korogase
  CHIIPA CHIIPA CHIIPACHIIPA
  TAMASHII korogase OKERA mo naiteita

Roll over the spirit, chiipa chiipa chiipachiipa.
Roll over the spirit, even a mole cricket was chirping.


  Translation: Robert Goodwin
