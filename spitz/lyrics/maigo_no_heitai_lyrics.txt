Lost troops  In a thorn they wave a black flag

--
 
  uchiotoseru kumo ni doujou shite wa
  atari no nai KUJI wo hikitsuzuke
  shigamitsuku tori wo sagashiteiru owarinaki tabi

Feeling sympathy for the cloud they can shoot down
they continue pulling up nails that missed it.
They are searching for a bird to cling to.  It's a neverending journey.

Wipe out a single trickle that flows.
As for taking refuge, it is always done inside a cell.
Even a timid smiling face becomes unstuck.  It's a thinned ball of fire.
